EXPLORE DUBAI...

Dubai, United Arab Emirates, is found on the Arabian Peninsula's eastern coast in the Persian Gulf. Dubai was one of seven Emirates. Abu Dhabi, Fujairah, Ajman, Umm al-Quwain, Sharjah, and Ras al-Khaimah are among them. Dubai's most fascinating feature is Dubai Creek, which divides the city into two regions: Bur Dubai and Deira. Bur Dubai refers to the southern area, where commercial and residential areas emerge, whereas Deira refers to the region north of the Creek. Dubai is well-known for its stunning architectural design and man-made island, which people flock to see and enjoy the place and amenities.

Protestant, Catholic, Western, and Orthodox churches are present in Christian gathering places.
